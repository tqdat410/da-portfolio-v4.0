## Overview

Creative simulation product for a Development Philosophy course. The project models competing social interests in an interactive format and was later adopted as official course material.

## Key Features

- Interactive social-interest simulation
- AI-assisted research and content workflow
- Course-ready educational product quality

## Role

Owner and solo developer.

## Responsibilities

- Full concept and interaction design
- End-to-end development and release
- AI-assisted content research and generation

## Tech Stack

- Next.js
- TypeScript
- Supabase
- Tailwind CSS
- Cloudflare

## Results

- Positive lecturer evaluation
- Adopted as formal university course material

## Key Learnings

Strengthened game-logic thinking, educational storytelling, and fast solo product execution.
